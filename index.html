About Marcello

Marcello is from Denver, CO. Aside from learning to code and doing his best to go day by day, he is an author, filmmaker, and co-host of Shufflebox Podcast. His book Big Ball of Hurt detailed his stuggles with anxiety, depression, and growing up alongside constant death. He has associate produced "Don't Fuck In The Woods 2" and produced and starred in two short films, "Circle" (which he also directed) and "QuaranTEAM". Along with this, he has written screenplays such as "Hello :)" and "5 Lies" that have been finalists in various festival contests. He co-hosts the Shufflebox Podcast with his friend Alex "Big Dog" Vallejos which covers various topics from history to growing up, but mostly it's about movies. They recently interviewed baseball legend Rickey Hill and director Jeff Celentano about their film The Hill which covers Rickey's struggles with his health and faith on the road to becoming a baseball player.
